Our Pilots

Alexandra and Marina have over 15 years of experience in aviation.
Meet two brave pilots who have conquered the skies with their incredible talent and courage. The first, Alexandra, has dreamed of flying since childhood. Her fascination with the sky began with small toy planes that she lovingly collected. She grew up to become renowned for her unparalleled ability to maneuver even in the most challenging conditions. The second heroine, Marina, had a slightly different story. Her father was a famous pilot, and from an early age, Marina knew she would follow in his footsteps. She grew up among airplanes and aviation tools, and her knowledge and skills became legendary among her colleagues. Marina not only expertly piloted aircraft but also helped others learn to fly, being an excellent instructor and mentor.
In one of the world's largest airports, two passenger airline pilots were preparing for another flight. They had much in common: both chose the profession of a pilot due to their passion for the sky and aviation, and both knew that each flight was not just a job but a responsibility for the lives of hundreds of passengers. Oleksandr, a captain with twenty years of experience, was a veteran of aviation. He began his career in small regional airlines, gradually climbing the career ladder. Oleksandr was known among his colleagues for his calm nature and confidence in every decision. His experience included flights to various parts of the world, from hot deserts to snowy mountainous regions. Next to him sat the young first officer Andrii, who had only five years of experience but had already established himself as a talented and responsible pilot.
Two passenger airline pilots, experienced captain Oleksandr with twenty years of service and young, talented first officer Andrii.
In a major international airport worked Serhiy, a highly experienced pilot who dedicated over thirty years of his life to aviation.
At a major international airport, there worked a highly experienced pilot named Serhiy. He dedicated over thirty years of his life to aviation, accumulating invaluable experience and knowledge. Serhiy began his career in military aviation, where he gained unparalleled piloting skills in challenging conditions. After completing his service, he transitioned to commercial aviation, where he quickly became one of the most respected captains. His calm demeanor, confidence, and ability to make quick decisions in critical situations always impressed his colleagues and passengers. Over the years, Serhiy visited hundreds of airports worldwide, experiencing diverse cultures and landscapes. His professional journey was filled with both routine flights and extraordinary situations, which he always handled with honor and skill.
